Welcome To Our Website

THE HEART OF GIVERS

THE HEART OF GIVERS is an NGO that is situated in Thohoyandou, limpopo. It cares for those who can not do for themselves by providing them with food, sanitary towels clothes,and stationary

Heart of givers organisation is an organisation that was esatablished with the aim of helping the needy. This organisation has helped thousands of people across Limpopo. It is an organisation that take cares of those who can not do for themselves. However,It also helps on motivating young generation in terms of academy and life challenges

What we do in our organisation?

We give sanitary towels to all females who struggle to buy for themselves, we do hand food to families that are poor, also we do career tour for matric students who wants guidance regarding career wise. Not all youth gets chance to have someone to talk to them about education & life challenges,Which is why we also do some motivational speaking to people who needs someone who has experienced life before. .

  • Best life talk ever?

    • We give motivational talk to those who are hopeless with life , to have hope again and believe on what they do and also to improve better.

  • Are we strictly focusing on empowering the youth of TODAY ?

    • We aim to empower all the youth of venda and the surrounding areas to grow big with full of potential and courage, this is how we can improve the mindset of our upcoming-GENERATION.
