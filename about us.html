Welcome To

HEART OF GIVERS ORGANISATION

The HEART OF GIVERS ORGANISATION is the number one rated organisation in limpopo in terms of lending hand to needy.

MISSION

Our mission as Heart of Givers Organisation is to make sure no child feels unloved, shuttered ,while we can help. We provide every child who is helpless by offering them free food, shelters and clothes. In additional,we also build houses to families that can not afford one. During exams,we also help students who can not complete application for university on their own.In additional, we also deals with trauma and all mind difficulties.

HISTORY

Heart of givers organisation has once distributed school stationary to more than 17 school ever since it was founded since 2018. A family once came through to us after their house was burnt down by natural disaster.We did fundraising and managed to raise money that built a house for the family. However,as we are still facing the challenge of Corona Virus, some families are still struggling to have food on their table as they are currently not working because of the pandemic, we have distributed food parcels to such families. Families that are over 14 in number.

VISION

The ideology of our vision is to continue to serve the community like never before,and set the highest standards of quality service and in that way, justify and uphold the reputation that we have among people of our community. Changing every poor's child by providing the necessary , giving them love and care.
